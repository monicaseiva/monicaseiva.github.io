SEU JORGE
Musicas para Churrasco II

2015 - 10 músicas, 44min 18s

The CD “Seu Jorge - Música Para Churrasco II” arrives four years after the release of the first album of the series, which was in 2011. The success of the album Música para Churrasco Vol 1 was great, and this encouraged the artists to give continuation for the parties with the new songs for Churrasco Vol 2. The album, like the first, brings 10 new songs, including “Motoboy” which is already a hit on all radio stations in Brazil. The cover was made by artist Vik Muniz.
